Patch 3684

Balance Update
May 27th, 2017 Released

Included in this patch are a veterancy overhaul, factory HP changes (yes, again), a Seraphim second gun buff, reduced wreck reclaim rate and more. This update brings significant adjustments to mobile missile launchers, Aurora accuracy while moving, factory health values across all tech levels and factions, ACU enhancements including the Seraphim Refracting Chronotron Amplifier and Aeon Personal Shield Generator, plus important fixes to the Janus fighter-bomber and various experimental units.

⚖️ The Balance Team
