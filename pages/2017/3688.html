Patch 3688

Balance Update
September 24th, 2017 Released

This balance patch will be a small one. The main feature of this patch is the move to a mass-based veterancy system. We have also scrapped changes to the shield spillover and overcharge for now since we want to do them either after or with the next patch. We hope you can appreciate the changes we made with this patch and welcome constructive feedback via the forum.

We wish you good luck and much fun playing with the new patch!

⚖️ The Balance Team
