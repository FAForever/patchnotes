Galactic Colossus

Nerf

Previously the claws would instantaneously destroy the unit upon arrival. This matched the old behavior, but with the bug fixes of the claws it was only slightly too strong.

ALL FACTIONS
Buff

When they were moved from tech 2 to tech 1 their build time was not reduced accordingly.

Economy

  • Build Time: 450 350

ACUs

Chrono Dampener

AEON
Nerf

The chrono dampener received several buffs over the past year. With this patch we nerf it by changing how the Chrono Dampener behaves.

Other

  • Old Behavior: Applies the same stun on all units over a large radius every 5.0 seconds. The stun duration was 3.5 seconds.
  • New Behavior: Applies a diminishing stun based on the distance of the unit to the ACU over a large radius every 5.0 seconds. The stun is applied in slices of 0.4 seconds. There are 10 slices. Units that are on top of the ACU receive all slices, and are therefore stunned for 4.0 seconds. Units at the outer edge of the radius receive only a single slice and are therefore stunned for 0.4 seconds.
