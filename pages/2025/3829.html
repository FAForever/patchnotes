Patch 3825

Balance Update
August 15th, 2025 Released

This patch focuses on targeted balance adjustments across land, naval, and ACU enhancement systems. The Cybran Sky Slammer T1 mobile AA receives a damage reduction to account for its improved projectile retargeting capabilities. Naval balance sees changes with the UEF Bulwark shield boat and Cybran CI:18 Mermaid stealth boat both receiving size reductions while the CI:18 Mermaid also gets a durability reduction to improve counterplay opportunities. The Seraphim Sou-istle T1 submarine's anti-torpedo defense reload is nerfed to prevent excessive dominance against other T1 subs and snowballing effects. ACU enhancement costs are rebalanced, with the first Aeon range upgrade becoming more expensive while the speed upgrades costs are reduced, and Cybran torpedo upgrade gains improved salvo size and the nano repair loses some regen rate.

Fire Beetle

XRL0302
Cybran
Buff

Beetles now consume 10 Energy per second while cloaked. Previously the only cloaked unit without an energy upkeep, they enabled low-cost early T2 rushes despite their high power. This change aligns them with other cloaked units and slightly curbs early aggression without compromising their core role.

Economy

  • Energy Drain: 0 10

Megalith

XRL0403
Cybran
Buff

The Megalith has received several usability improvements to streamline egg deployment and engineering behavior. Its build range has been extended by +1, allowing eggs to be placed farther away and preventing them from spawning within the Megalith’s footprint, which previously caused unnecessary movement. Repair capability has been added so the Megalith can finish incomplete eggs, and reclaim functionality is now explicitly enabled—whereas before, it could only reclaim by placing eggs on wrecks. Pause functionality has also been added to bring it in line with standard engineering units. Lastly, the stats for T3 Mobile Artillery and T3 Mobile Anti-Air crab eggs have been updated to reflect recent balance changes to those units

Shocker

UAA0304
Aeon
Buff

Improve the maneuverability and reliability of Strategic Bombers to promote proactive use and compete with T3 gunships. Enhanced turn speed and lift factor allow strats to micro more effectively to avoid flak damage and reduce opportunities for ASF/SAMs to deal damage. The improved bomb drop threshold greatly increases reliability on uneven terrain or during turns, though bombs may drop from further away, making ACU dodging easier.
