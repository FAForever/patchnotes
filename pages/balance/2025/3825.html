Patch 3825

Balance Update
August 15th, 2025 Released

This patch delivers major balance improvements across multiple systems. The UEF's Cooper torpedo boat is buffed with reduced cost, improved sonar, and slightly higher DPS, helping address UEF navy’s T2 weaknesses. T3 artillery for all factions is rebalanced with adjusted costs, accuracy, and damage to ensure fairer performance against shielded targets. Additional tweaks fix air unit targeting bugs, improve teleport balance, enhance Cybran naval missile defense, and refine units like the Othuum, Solace, and Valiant for better counterplay and consistency.

⚖️ The Balance Team

Naval Units

Bulwark

XES0205
UEF
Nerf

Reduce the Bulwark's oversized dimensions to better reflect its current role and cost efficiency. Previous FAF changes significantly increased the unit's scale, making it nearly as large as a destroyer despite being a relatively inexpensive support vessel. With the Bulwark's recent rework emphasizing affordability over raw effectiveness, its massive size no longer aligns with its tactical purpose. These adjustments restore appropriate proportions for a cost-effective T2 support unit while maintaining its core shielding functionality.

CI:18 Mermaid

XRS0205
Cybran
Nerf

Reduce the Mermaid's excessive size and durability to promote better counterplay opportunities and prevent it from becoming overpowered. Previous FAF changes dramatically increased the unit's scale, making this relatively affordable support vessel nearly as large as a destroyer. The Mermaid's current combination of high HP-to-mass ratio and oversized dimensions makes it too resilient for its cost, limiting strategic options for opponents. These adjustments scale back both its physical presence and survivability to create more tactical vulnerability while preserving its core counter-intelligence capabilities.

ACUs & SACUs

Enhanced Quantum Disruptor:

Enhancement
Aeon
Nerf

Rebalance the Aeon ACU's first range upgrade costs to create strategic parity with other factions' gun upgrade options. The increased investment required for the range upgrade ensures that combined range and speed upgrades match the total cost of other factions' comprehensive gun improvements. This change also equalizes the individual costs of speed and range upgrades, preventing players from exploiting cost disparities to rush specific upgrades on smaller maps where early positioning advantages can be decisive.

Economy

  • Mass Cost: 300 400
  • Energy Cost: 6000 12000
  • Build Time: 350 400

Quantum Accelerator

Enhancement
Aeon
Buff

Reduce the fire rate upgrade costs to balance the overall investment required for Aeon ACU enhancement paths. By lowering the fire rate upgrade costs to match the new range upgrade pricing, both individual upgrades now require equal investment, preventing strategic exploitation where players could prioritize cheaper upgrades for early game advantages. This adjustment ensures that Aeon players must make meaningful tactical decisions between range and fire rate improvements without cost-based bias influencing their upgrade priorities on smaller maps.

Economy

  • Mass Cost: 500 400
  • Energy Cost: 15000 12000
  • Build Time: 500 400

Nanite Torpedo Launcher

Enhancement
Cybran
Buff

Improve the Cybran ACU's Nanite Torpedo system's ability to overcome enemy torpedo defenses by increasing the salvo size from 3 to 4 torpedoes per burst. Despite previous improvements in patch #6476 that increased the salvo size to 3, the upgrade continued to struggle against well-defended naval positions with multiple torpedo defense systems. This enhancement maintains the same overall damage per second while delivering torpedoes in larger volleys, significantly improving penetration capability against layered torpedo defenses and making the upgrade more viable in contested naval engagements.

Weapon

  • Damage (DPS): 60 (225) 45 (225)
  • Muzzle Salvo Size: 3 4
