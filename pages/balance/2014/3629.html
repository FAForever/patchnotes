Patch 3629

Balance Update
February 2nd, 2014 Released

Patch 3629 delivers a broad set of balance changes for Supreme Commander: Forged Alliance Forever, refining unit stats and abilities across land, air, naval, ACU, and SCU categories. The update aims to improve gameplay fairness, unit diversity, and strategic counterplay by adjusting damage, speed, veterancy, and enhancement costs. Key highlights include tweaks to mobile artillery, bombers, gunships, submarines, and commander upgrades.

⚖️ The Balance Team
