Patch 3810

Balance Update
July 7th, 2024 Released

This balance update comprises numerous minor adjustments to certain units, providing them with backup capabilities at a distance. Additionally, there are a few significant changes, such as the introduction of the new Teleport mechanic, alongside some slight nerfs.

Cougar

DELK002
UEF
Buff

The UEF’s T3 Mobile Anti-Air has been upgraded with higher damage output and improved DPS, giving it stronger performance against high-value air units. With its new speed, it can now better accompany Titans and Parashields in the field. This adjustment allows the unit to bring down a strategic bomber in just 3 salvos instead of 4, making it a more reliable frontline anti-air option.

Physics

  • Speed: 3.3 3.5

Weapons

  • Damage Per Volley: 1200 1400
  • Salvo Size: 12 14
  • Reload Time: 4.5 4.3

Redeemer

DALK003
Aeon
Buff

The Aeon Redeemer has had its movement speed reduced, trading mobility with the UEF’s T3 MAA to help balance inter-faction diversity. Despite being slightly slower, Redeemer retains its core effectiveness as a mobile anti-air platform. Since Aeon already benefits from Asylums’ unusually high speed (4.0) and Harbingers’ relatively low speed (3.0), Redeemer’s reduced mobility has minimal impact on Aeon’s overall unit synergy.

Physics

  • Speed: 3.5 3.3

Experimental Artillery

Salvation

After being fixed a long time ago, the adjacency bonus made Salvation more effective than Mavor as a game ending artillery as recently discovered. Since Salvation's purpose is to be spammed en masse after a Paragon, and not to be built independently as a superior artillery piece, the adjacency bonus that makes building Salvation without Paragon overpowered is nerfed.
