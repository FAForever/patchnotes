Patch 3818

Balance Update
February 14th, 2025 Released

This patch brings a wide range of balance adjustments aimed at refining both land and naval gameplay. Key highlights include a major rework to the Vulthoo gunship for earlier viability, buffs to the underused Czar experimental, and improved micro-responsiveness for sniper bots. Naval units like the Seraphim destroyer and submarine hunter receive nerfs to promote better counterplay, while anti-torpedo systems are upgraded with smarter targeting. Additional fixes improve projectile behavior, shield assisting, and pathing consistency for better gameplay clarity.

⚖️ The Balance Team

Patch 3801

Date: March 15 2024

This balance update comprises numerous minor adjustments to certain units, providing them with backup capabilities at a distance. Additionally, there are a few significant changes, such as the introduction of the new Teleport mechanic, alongside some slight nerfs.
