Structures

T4 Arty (Salvation)

Small Nerf to the Salvation to be close to the balance back in 2016

The drain is only increased so that the charging can actually keep up with the reload time, and it has no relevant balance consequences outside of needing 2 energy storages to fill in between charges.

  • HP: 10000→ 9000
  • EnergyDrainPerSecond: 10000 → 10714
  • MinRadius: 175 → 150
  • MuzzleChargeDelay: 0.15 → 0.2
  • MuzzleVelocity: 150 → 98
  • MuzzleVelocityReduceDistance: 4000 → 1400
  • RackRecoilDistance: -0.5 → -0.625

Mechanics

Teleport

Teleport now scales energy quadraticly so the further the distance you want to go the more Energy it will cost.

Example:

  • 50 = 50 * 50 = 2,500
  • 100 = 100 * 100 = 10,000
