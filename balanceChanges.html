-- Balance Team

Navy

T3 Missle Ship

This change totally reworks the role of the Torrent from a expensive battleship level unit to a cheap AOE missile ship. This should make Torrents viable in more situations.

  • Health: 15000 → 7500
  • EnergyCost: 80000 → 40000
  • MassCost: 8000 → 4000
  • Damage: 800 → 200
  • DamageRadius: 2 → 3
  • SalvoDelay: 1 → 0.6
  • SalvoSize: 5 → 10

ACUs

UEF Teleport

Allows for teleport to be used with billy nuke, while not allowing for the ACU to also have a T3 upgrade.

  • Slot: Back → Left Arm
